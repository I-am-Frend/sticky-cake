What is a sticky cake?

It is of course a pastry, a cake baked with love and care. Resulting in what one could only describe as a sticky goodness, it goes hand in hand with coffee. Making it a fantastic choice if you have a relatives or friends over. So get that coffee ready and let's get to baking.

Why a sticky cake?

Mostly because it pairs well with the bitterness from coffee, it's also because it's a Traditional swedish cake dating back to the 1970's where it was invented, and became a staple cake.
